About the System

The IoT Based Health Monitoring System continuously measures vital signs such as body temperature and heart rate using sensors like DHT22 and Pulse Sensor. It then sends the data wirelessly through an ESP8266 NodeMCU to the cloud (ThingSpeak), allowing for live tracking and early health detection.

Doctor Remote Monitoring

With this system, doctors can monitor their patients from anywhere in the world. The live dashboard gives real-time access to patients’ heart rate and temperature, enabling quick decision-making and early intervention — even from home. The system also allows online consultations between doctors and patients for efficient, continuous care.

Health Tracking in Gyms

The same technology can be applied in gyms and fitness centers to monitor gym members’ heart rate and body temperature during workouts. Trainers can use real-time data to ensure safety, avoid overexertion, and help clients maintain a healthy rhythm. This makes the system ideal for both healthcare and fitness industries.
