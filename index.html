About the Project

This IoT-based Health Monitoring System uses Arduino Uno, ESP8266 NodeMCU, Pulse Sensor, DHT22 Sensor, and LCD Display. The system collects heart rate (BPM), temperature, and humidity readings, sends them to ThingSpeak, and displays real-time data on a professional dashboard. Doctors can monitor patients remotely and provide online consultations, while gyms can track members' heart performance during workouts.
