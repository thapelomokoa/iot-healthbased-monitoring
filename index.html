About Us

Our IoT Health Monitoring System tracks real-time heart rate, temperature, and humidity using IoT sensors, Arduino, ESP8266, and cloud services.

Technologies Used: Arduino, ESP8266 NodeMCU, DHT22 Sensor, Pulse Sensor, ThingSpeak, Chart.js, AI Insights

Mission: To provide easy-to-access health monitoring for patients at home, gyms, and clinics, with real-time dashboards and telemedicine support.
