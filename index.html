About Our System

Our IoT-based health monitoring system uses Arduino UNO, ESP8266 (NodeMCU), DHT22 sensor, and Pulse Sensor to collect vital signs such as temperature and heart rate. Data is sent to ThingSpeak for live analysis, allowing doctors and gyms to track wellness remotely.
